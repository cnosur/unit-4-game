Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number to start the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated. You lose the game if your total score above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystals.
